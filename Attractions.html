Augmented Reality Attractions

Take your virtual experience to the next level with interactive augmented reality adventures! Completely replace your real-world environement with the latest in haptic mult-sensory technology. Feel as if you are in the action and forge your own path with multiple storylines and environements.

Where the journey begins: Platform 9 3/4

Take your family to Platform Nine and Three-Quarters, hidden from view between platforms 9 and 10. Ride the Hogwarts Express and start your own wizrding adventure!

Visit the Ministry of Magic

Join the Minister from Magic as he informs the Muggle Prime Minister of the existance of the wizarding world in this exciting visit to the Ministry of Magic

Venture deep into the Forbidden Forest

Join Hagrid the Gamekeeper at the edge of the Forbidden Forest where many creatures dark, dangerous and friendly call home

Magical creatures exhibition

At night, Tree roots and stumps at the edge of the Dark Forest are barely visible in near blackness. Here, many magical creatures roam and perform incredible acts of magic

Visit Hogwarts school

Enroll in the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and put on the sorting hat to begin your journey as a young wizard

Have a walk in Diagon Alley

Just behind the Leaky Couldron pub, the cobblestoned wizarding alley completely hidden from Muggles is a wizarding community with characters of all shapes and sizes. Come join in the fun and play some bar games with fellow wizards!
